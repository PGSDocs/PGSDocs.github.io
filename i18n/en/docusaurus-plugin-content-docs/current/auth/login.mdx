# <i class="fas fa-sign-in-alt"></i> Logging into your account
![INSCRIPTION](../img/register.png)

Logging into your account on **PRO GESTION SOFT** is designed to be quick, secure, and simple. If you are already registered, you can easily log in at `http://progestionsoft.com/login` using your email address and password.

Here’s a detailed guide to help you through this step, as well as the options available in case you forget your password.


## Login steps
To access your account, follow the steps below:

-	Enter your **email address**: Make sure to enter the email address you used during registration. Ensure you enter it correctly, as even a small mistake can prevent you from logging in.
-	Enter your **password**: The password must match the one you set during registration. For security reasons, your password is masked with symbols during entry to protect your information from prying eyes.
-	Click the **"Login"** button: Once you have entered your credentials, click the Login button to submit your information. We will verify that the information matches an existing account. If correct, you will be redirected to your account homepage.


## In case you forget your password 
We understand that it is possible to forget your password. To facilitate recovery of access to your account, we have implemented a simple and secure password reset procedure. Here’s how to proceed:

-	Click on **"Forgot Password"** on the login page, just below the "Login" button to initiate the reset process.
-	Enter your **email address**: A new page will open, inviting you to enter the email address associated with your account. This step is crucial as it allows us to verify your identity by sending a six-digit code to a verified address.
-	Receive the **verification code**: Once your email address is submitted, we will send you an email containing a six-digit code to reset your password. This code is valid for 15 minutes.
-	Create a **new password**: By entering the code sent via email into the input field, you will be directed to a page where you can choose a new password. Make sure to create a strong password by following the security recommendations (a minimum of eight characters, including uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols).
-	**Log in** with the new password: Once your password is reset, you can return to the login page and use your new email and password combination to access your account.

:::tip ***Tips for ensuring the security of your account***
To maintain a high level of security for your account, here are some best practices to adopt:
-	***Change your password regularly***: Regularly updating your password helps limit the risk of your account being compromised.
-	***Do not share your credentials***: Keep your password private and avoid sharing it with others, even those you trust.
-	***Use a password manager***: If you have difficulty remembering a complex password, consider using a secure password manager to store and protect your information.
-	***Monitor for suspicious logins***: If you notice unusual activity on your account, such as logins you did not perform, contact our support team immediately to take necessary actions.
:::
