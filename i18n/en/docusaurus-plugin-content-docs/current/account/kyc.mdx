Congratulations! You have successfully created an account on **PRO GESTION SOFT** and can now access it.  

To ensure the authenticity of the information provided and to secure your account, a KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required. This procedure validates your identity and the legal information of your company.


## Why KYC verification?  
KYC verification aims to protect users by ensuring that each account belongs to a verified and authentic entity. This step not only meets legal requirements but also ensures data security on the platform by limiting access to authenticated users only. Thus, KYC verification is essential for full and secure access to the advanced features of Pro Gestion Soft.


## Let’s get started  
We will assist you in verifying your account in a few simple steps and tracking the status of this verification.

### Steps to verify your account
:::warning
You can only submit a KYC verification request ***once every 30 days***. Please ensure that all documents provided are up-to-date, legitimate, and that the information is accurate.
:::

To complete the verification of your account, please follow these detailed steps:

-	Log in to your account.
-	In the upper right corner of your interface, click on the profile icon <i class="fas fa-user-circle"></i> (or your profile picture if you have changed it at least once) to open the dropdown menu.
-	Select « **Expand** » to access your Profile Space.
-	Navigate to the **DOCUMENTS** section where you can initiate the verification process. You will see fields for the required information and documents, including:
    - *[IFU document](/intro.mdx#Terminologie)*: Upload a copy of your Unique Tax Identification Number (IFU), which identifies your business for tax purposes.
    - *[Trade register](/intro.mdx#Terminologie)*: A copy of your trade register, confirming the legal existence of your business.
    - Manager’s ID: This document validates the identity of the person responsible for the account.
-	Once the information and documents are provided, click Save to submit the items.

:::tip Tips for document validation
-	***Ensure document quality***: Submitted documents must be clearly legible. Avoid blurry, cropped, or poorly framed photos, as this could delay validation. Prefer scans or clear photographs, and ensure that all important details are visible.
-	***Check size and format***: Files must be in PDF, JPEG, or PNG format, with a maximum size of 5 MB per document. Ensure that the file format is correct to avoid upload failures.
-	***Update information***: In case of a change of manager or updates to company documents, it is important to submit recent versions. This update keeps your account compliant and avoids any restrictions.
:::

###	Tracking Verification Status
> KYC verification may take between 24 and 72 hours. Once approved, you will receive a confirmation email.

After submitting your documents, we will verify your information and send you a notification email to confirm that verification is in progress. 

You can track the status of your verification by returning to the DOCUMENTS section of your profile.

:::note
-	***Data confidentiality***: All documents submitted as part of the KYC verification are strictly confidential. Pro Gestion Soft adheres to a strict privacy policy, and no document will be shared with third parties without your consent.
-	***Account security***: This step is crucial for ensuring the security and validity of your account. Without KYC verification, access to certain features of the platform may be restricted or even suspended.
:::
