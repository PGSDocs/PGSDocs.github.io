# <i class="fas fa-hand"></i> Welcome
Welcome to the documentation for **[PRO GESTION SOFT](https://progestionsoft.com), an integrated management platform specifically designed to meet the needs of Beninese and African SMEs.


## Objective
This documentation aims to guide you step by step in using and configuring of the platform while providing practical advice and solutions to common problems. This all-in-one tool, designed to simplify internal operations for businesses, allows for the centralization of key functions such as email management, human resources, document management, recruitment processes, as well as sales and purchases.
Whether you are an administrator, employee, or HR manager, this documentation will provide you with all the necessary information to fully leverage the features of the platform.
